Overview

概述

Fiscalia is a local-first personal spending tracker. All data you enter is stored locally on your device.

Fiscalia 是一款本機優先的個人支出追蹤 App。你輸入的所有資料都只儲存在你的裝置本機。

開發者

What data Fiscalia handles

Fiscalia 會處理哪些資料

Fiscalia may store the information you manually enter, such as spending records, categories, budgets, recurring spending, notes, and related app settings.

Fiscalia 可能會儲存你手動輸入的資訊，例如支出紀錄、分類、預算、定期支出、備註，以及相關 App 設定。

How your data is used

你的資料如何被使用

Your data is used only to provide the app’s spending-tracking and budgeting features on your device.

你的資料僅用於在你的裝置上提供支出追蹤與預算管理功能。

No data collection or tracking

不蒐集資料或進行追蹤

Fiscalia does not collect, transmit, or store your app data on our servers.

Fiscalia 不會將你的 App 資料蒐集、傳送或儲存在我們的伺服器上。

  • No analytics or tracking technologies are used.
  • No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.
  • 不需要建立帳號。

Import and export

匯入與匯出

Fiscalia supports CSV import and export only when you choose to use those features. Your data is not shared automatically.

Fiscalia 僅會在你主動使用相關功能時支援 CSV 匯入與匯出。你的資料不會被自動分享。

Temporary safety snapshots

暫時安全快照

To help protect your data during certain major changes, such as home currency changes, CSV imports, or some bulk edits, Fiscalia may create a temporary local “safety snapshot” on your device before the change is applied.

為了在某些較大的資料變更期間提供額外保護，例如主要貨幣變更、CSV 匯入或部分批次編輯， Fiscalia 可能會在套用變更前，於你的裝置本機建立一份暫時的「數據快照」。

These temporary safety snapshots are stored locally on your device only, are not sent to our servers, and are removed, replaced, or allowed to expire automatically over time.

這些暫時數據快照只會儲存在你的裝置本機，不會傳送到我們的伺服器，並會隨時間自動移除、 被覆寫或到期失效。

Local data and backups

本機資料與備份

All data is stored locally on your device, including any temporary local safety snapshots used for recovery during certain major changes. If the app is removed, app data is deleted, or your device is lost, reset, or damaged, your data may also be lost unless you have your own backup.

所有資料皆儲存在你的裝置本機，包括在某些較大變更期間用於復原的暫時本機數據快照。 若 App 被移除、App 資料遭刪除，或你的裝置遺失、重置或損壞，相關資料也可能一併遺失， 除非你已自行備份。

本政策的變更

若未來 Fiscalia 的功能或資料處理方式有所變更，本隱私權政策也可能更新。
