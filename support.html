About Fiscalia

關於 Fiscalia

Fiscalia is a local-first personal finance app for tracking spending, budgets, and recurring expenses. No account is required, and all user data is stored locally on the device.

Fiscalia 是一款本機優先的個人理財 App，用於追蹤支出、管理預算與定期支出。無需帳號，所有使用者資料皆儲存在裝置本機。
