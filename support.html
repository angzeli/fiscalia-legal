Before you contact support

聯絡支援前

If you are reporting a bug, it helps to include your app version, device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

如果你要回報 Bug，建議附上 App 版本、裝置型號、iOS 版本，以及問題的簡短說明。

Local-first data note

本機資料說明

Fiscalia is designed to store data locally on your device. This means support cannot directly view or recover your personal spending data.

Fiscalia 採本機儲存設計，因此支援方無法直接查看或恢復你的個人支出資料。

Response expectations

回覆說明

Support requests are handled directly by the developer. Replies may take a few business days.

支援請求由開發者直接處理，回覆可能需要數個工作天。

About Fiscalia

關於 Fiscalia

Fiscalia is a local-first personal spending tracker designed to help you understand your spending, manage budgets, and stay organised without requiring an account.

Fiscalia 是一款本機優先的個人支出追蹤 App，幫助你了解支出、管理預算，並在不需要帳號的情況下維持清楚有序的財務紀錄。
