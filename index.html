Fiscalia is a local-first personal finance app. All spending data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device.

Fiscalia may store the information you manually enter, such as spending records, categories, budgets, recurring expenses, and related settings.

How your data is used

Your data is used only to provide the app’s budgeting and spending-tracking functionality on your device.

Data sharing
