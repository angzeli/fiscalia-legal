Privacy Policy for Fiscalia

Effective date: 30 March 2026

Fiscalia is a local-only personal finance app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device.

What data Fiscalia collects

Fiscalia stores the financial information you manually enter into the app, such as spending records, categories, budgets, and recurring expenses.

How your data is used

Your data is used only to provide the app’s budgeting and spending-tracking functionality.

Data sharing

Fiscalia does not require account creation, does not use cloud sync, does not show ads, does not use tracking, and does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Data storage

All data is stored locally on your device.
